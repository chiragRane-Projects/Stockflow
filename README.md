## ğŸ“ Features
- RBAC Auth: Using jwt this system consists of secured role based auth. 
- Inventory: Basic CRUD operations with add ons of stock reorder reminder.
- Orders: Sales CRUD which helps to generate some analytics regarding business.
- Cash Drawer: Calculated closing cash on the basis of cash expenses throughout the day. Currently it calculates closing amount on basis of both cash and UPI payments.
- Reports: Three types of reports get generated viz Sales report, Expenses report, Profit-Loss report, Stock report. Reports are generated using recharts.
